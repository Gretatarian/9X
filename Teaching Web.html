Ls-1 Locating Places on a Globe

The Types of Lines in globe

There are two types of Imaginary Lines in the Earth called Latitude and Longitude.Used to Find Places.We can use those imaginary lines to locate places on a globe.

Latitude

Lines of Latitude are imaginary lines run in the east-west direction.The Earth is divided into half as Northern Hemisphere and Southern Hemisphere.Some of important lines in the Latitude are Equator present at the 0° Latitude.Tropic of Cancer at 23.5°N,Tropic of Capicorn at 23.5°S,Arctic Circle AT 66.5°N,Antartic Circle at 66.5°S,North Pole 90°N,South Pole 90°S.
